Rubber Pavement

  • Crack resistant & able to resist minor ground movement
  • Durable & seamless surface
  • Slip resistant
  • Does not deteriorate when used with salt/de-icer solutions
  • Withstands climate changes & freeze/thaw cycles
  • Easy snow removal
  • Affordable alternative to traditional concrete or asphalt

Permamulch

Permamulch is a alternative solution to any landscaping project. This landscaping solution will stay in place and not shift. Providing a smoother mancured look during all times. This will also keep up with intense water pressure so it able to be washed with power-washers and other cleaning methods.

Resurfacing

Have a surface that you don't want to spend the time to rip it all out before putting a rubber solution down. Our rubber pavement solution can be applied over old surfaces to provide a like new look and durability.

Walkways

Walkways and entrypaths can by done to give a more slip resistant surace to walk on. the pavement being porous prevents water from pooling ontop of the surface. Handy for pools, hot tubs, wash pads, entry ways, and garden paths.

Patios

Patios can fade over time and can be annowying to clean without taking polish off or digging into the sorface. With rubber you can rest easy knowing that cleaning the patio is simple, easy, and intended.
